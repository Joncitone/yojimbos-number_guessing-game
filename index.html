Yojimbo's Number

You have encountered Yojimbo while adventuring in Eorzea. Beware his awesome blade!

In order to continue on your journey you must guess Yojimbo's number.

He's thinking of a number from a min of 1 to a max of 100.

Upon submitting guesses, Yojimbo will give you clues to let you know if you're close or far from his number.

You may ask for a hint during the game, or you may choose to play again.

You have five guesses.
